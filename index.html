About Me

A Trekkie at Heart

Currently a Marketing Manager at the University of New Hampshire, Megan has had a career that has given her experience in a variety of areas. Starting out as a media arts major at Long Island University - Brooklyn Campus, Megan worked with employees of NBC's production crew that moonlit as faculty during their free-time.

Megan then relocated to New Hampshire and applied her expertise in branding, design, and editing to digtial marketing for higher education, where she discovered her passion for webdesign and user experience. Megan's ultimate goal is to help clients create the best experience for their customers, ultimately so they return and become life-long clientele.

Currently pursing a Certificate in Full Stack Web Development from the University of New Hampshire through Trilogy, Megan's skills included HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Her strengths include project management, creativity, and leading teams. She is certified in Lean Six Sigma and as a Scrum Master. She successfully migrated the Career & Professional Success website to an upgraded developer platform while restructuring navigation and reducing redundancies to meet best practices in SEO and UX/I, which has allowed site visitors to find services and resources quickly.
